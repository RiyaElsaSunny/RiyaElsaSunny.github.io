ERNAKULAM

District in Kerala, India

Ernakulam is a vibrant city in Kerala, India, that offers a blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and modern amenities. Whether you want to explore the historic Hill Palace Museum, admire the art and technology at the Madhavan Nayar Foundation, or visit the birthplace of the Hindu philosopher Adi Shankara at Kaladi, Ernakulam has something for everyone. You can also enjoy the scenic views of the Periyar River, the lush greenery of the Thattekkad Bird Sanctuary, or the serene atmosphere of the Malayatoor Church. Ernakulam is a great base to explore the nearby Kochi, the commercial and cultural hub of Kerala, where you can experience the colonial charm, the cosmopolitan lifestyle, and the delicious cuisine of the region.
